Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Information We Collect

Cliperton is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • No Clipboard Data: Your clipboard content stays on your device and is never transmitted to our servers
  • Download Statistics: When you download Cliperton, we log basic information like download time and general location for statistical purposes
  • Crash Reports: If the application crashes, anonymous technical data may be collected to help us improve the software

How We Use Information

Any information collected is used solely to:

  • Provide download statistics and usage analytics
  • Improve the application's stability and performance
  • Respond to technical support requests

Data Storage and Security

Your clipboard data is stored locally on your device only. We implement appropriate security measures to protect any information we do collect.

Third-Party Services

Our website may use analytics services to understand how visitors interact with our site. These services may collect anonymous usage data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: support@cliperton.com
